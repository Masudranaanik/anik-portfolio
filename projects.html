A collection of my practical networking and IT setup projects.

Custom Cat6 Crossover Cable Assembly (T568A to T568B)

In this project, I demonstrated my understanding of networking standards by creating a functional Cat6 Crossover Cable. By terminating one end with the T568A standard and the other with T568B, I enabled direct communication between two similar networking devices (e.g., PC-to-PC) without the need for a switch or hub. This showcases my manual crimping skills and theoretical knowledge of pin-out configurations.
